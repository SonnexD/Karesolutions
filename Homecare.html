    Home Care Service

    We are Karesolutions. Although, everyone needs support in one way or the other in life, admitting or realizing you may need help or support with day to day life exigencies can be a huge and sometimes life changing decision.

    The Karesolution Vision:

    “Every human has a right to live, and to live very comfortably regardless of race, gender and location. Whatever a client’s needs are; with the right support and care, we can all be empowered to live a fulfilled and dignified life".

    We provide fully trained care staff, recruited from a variety of backgrounds to help give the best support to our Clients in the comfort of their own homes. We always conduct and maintain a rigorous selection process with our carers using industry best practices and only engage the services of experts that demonstrates what we believe to be the highest moral standards and the ability to care for people in a professional and non-intrusive way.

    As a Homecare provider, we at Karesolutions take pride in our work and have huge respect for our carers and the work they do. Our carefully selected team of homecare professionals and coordinators will provide you with compelling care service experience.

    We listen to our clients and highly value any feedback that may help us to improve and develop our services. We pride ourselves on our innovative and personalized approach to care. Our innovative system and approach allows us to maintain smooth and efficient operations at all times. We offer a variety of flexible and affordable packages, from 30 minutes a day, up to 24 hours, all tailored to suit our customer’s needs.

    We Pride ourselves on giving you first class service with our team of experienced Nurses and Carers who offer Care to local communities as well as Nationwide. Whatever the need, karesolutions is your best bet to providing care in the home, tailored to your needs.

    We specialize in the following areas of Homecare Support:
    • Alzheimer's & Dementia
    • End of life Care
    • Sensory Impairment
    • Lifestyle Support (Social Care)
    • Physical Disabilities
    • Learning Difficulties
    • Personal care & help at home
    • Rehabilitation
    • Residential Supported Living
    • Brain Injury/Spinal Injury

    For further information about the structure of our homecare services, kindly get in touch with us today!
