    End-of-life Care Service

    End of life care means care for those who are approaching the end of their life, focused on comfort and wellbeing. If you're looking for palliative or end of life care, then Karesolutions is your sure professional care service provider.

    It can be a difficult and emotional experience when being told a friend or loved one’s illness is terminal. To then have to seek compassionate, trustworthy and efficient end of life care to help make their final days, weeks and months as comfortable and dignified as possible can be a daunting task.

    Karesolutions understands this and offers comprehensive end of life care services, often referred to as palliative care, to those who have advanced, terminal and progressive conditions. Our palliative care service can be put in place quickly and are always tailored to each customer’s needs; considering their symptoms, psychological wellbeing, social requirements or spiritual needs.

    Karesolutions always aims to:

    • Improve and enhance the client’s quality of life.
    • Help our customers live as actively and as independently as possible.
    • Use a team approach to address the needs of the customer and provide support to their families.
    • Combine and consider the emotional, social and spiritual aspects of the client’s care service.
    • Assist with the provision of relief from pain and other distressing symptoms.
    • Assist with the earlier stages of illness, alongside other therapies that are aimed at easing symptoms and to prolong life.
    • As always, Karesolutions will employ and carefully coordinate our most experienced care teams to meet your requirements.

    For further information about the structure of our End-of-life Care services, kindly get in touch with us today!
